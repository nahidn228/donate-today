    Our Donate Plan

    It is every man’s obligation to put back into the world at least the equivalent of what he takes out of it. ― Albert Einstein

    Food for a family

    Your donations can help us to support children affected by undernutrition, making sure that therapeutic milk is available at the nutrition facilities and ready for distribution. Your donation will help save lives.

    Shelter for a family

    Your donations can protect children and families against waterborne diseases through the immediate supply of hygiene kits, and water purification tablets that provide immediate relief on the ground.

    Education for a child

    Your donations also help us support continued education for children during and after floods and cyclones, providing learning support via makeshift schools, and distributing emergency learning kits.

    During any disaster or crisis, children are the most vulnerable Recurrent flooding, cyclones, and storm-surges are common events in Bangladesh that put millions of children at risk. Every year they leave vast devastation and damage in these areas, and we need to be ready to act. For more info

    Donate Today Bangladesh works on the ground to protect human and to help ensure the safety, health, nutrition, clean water, and education for those affected by floods and other disasters. When a disaster strikes, your donation will make a difference.
